1. The service

HQ (at h-q.io) is a computational deliberation service comprising four tools: RJE (ethical deliberation), Tetrad (a product-development review), Gresol (a pre-launch stress-test), and The Chamber (an ACI: artificial collective intelligence). You submit an artifact or a question; a panel of independent computational perspectives deliberates in parallel and returns a single written report.

HQ is sold for professional use: solo founders, teams, and organisations. By subscribing you confirm that you are acting for purposes relating to your trade, business, craft, or profession. 3. Plans, credits, and payment

The free trial includes three deliberation runs per account, with the limits shown on the pricing page. The trial does not require a payment method. We may limit the creation of multiple trial accounts by the same person. Paid access is by subscription (Solo or Pro, billed monthly or annually) or by enterprise agreement. Payments are processed by Stripe.

Usage is metered in credits. A deliberation run costs one credit; The Chamber costs two; large inputs cost proportionally more. The exact cost of a run is shown before you submit it. Subscription credits renew with each billing period and unused subscription credits do not roll over. Purchased top-up credits do not expire. If a run fails on our side, or is refused by our safety checks, the credits charged for it are returned to your account automatically, up to three automatic returns per account per day; beyond that, contact us and we will review and restore the credits manually.
