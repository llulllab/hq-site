The H4 method

H4 is not one system with one voice. It is a combinatorial parallel architecture: several independent reasoners take the same question, work it alone, then press on each other's disagreements. One voice writes the result.

01

Submit

You send an artifact or a question, and choose the lens. Nothing is trained on.

02

Independent positions

Several reasoners read it in parallel, each alone, so no single view dominates.

03

Cross-examination

They see each other's positions and press on the disagreements.

04

Structured report

One voice writes the result in minutes. You can leave and come back to it.

A deliberation takes one to three minutes. The report is the deliverable; you will not sit and watch a spinner.

The Chamber

An ACI: artificial collective intelligence

Not a model, not a framework: an ACI. For the questions one mind cannot settle. Independent minds reason your question together, each keeping its own character, and return where they converge, the strongest objection, and a considered view.

Use when you want the considered read of many strong minds, not one answer.

Gresol · pre-ship stress-test

Consumer health app, hero section

Verdict: do not ship
Blocker

The word "diagnosis" makes this a regulated medical device

"Diagnosis in seconds" is an intended-purpose statement. Under EU MDR 2017/745, software claiming diagnosis of disease is a medical device, placed at Class IIa or higher, and the page shows no conformity assessment. Fix: remove diagnosis language, or complete the MDR pathway before any EU traffic.

Blocker

Health-data consent is void as written

Bundling special-category-data consent into account creation fails GDPR Art. 9(2)(a) explicit consent and Art. 7 freely-given. "Our partners" is unnamed. Fix: unbundle into a separate, granular opt-in with named recipients and purposes.

Major

"100% accurate, trusted by doctors" is unsubstantiated

An absolute accuracy claim on a health product is a misleading action under UCPD Art. 6, and there is no substantiation file to produce. Fix: delete the accuracy figure and any blanket endorsement.

Full report ranks every finding, flags the panel's own likely over-fires, and names the single most likely real incident. This is a real Gresol output, lightly trimmed.
What happens to what you submit

Not used for training

Your submissions are used to produce your report and nothing else. They are not used to train any model.

Where it runs

RJE is processed and hosted in the EU. The other three tools run on a global panel; you are told before you submit, and you decide.

Retention you control

A report has a result link you can return to. You can delete a report and its content at any time.

Don't paste secrets you can't share

Treat a submission like sending a document to an outside reviewer. For code and stress-tests, leave out live credentials.
